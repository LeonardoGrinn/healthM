THE BASICS OF WEALTH TRANSFER

Passing on wealth is something for which most people plan. Having a well-thought-out wealth transfer plan may help your clients:

  • Find more efficient ways to increase the amount of wealth they leave to their heirs
  • Plan for an income tax-free death benefit
  • Plan for efficiently passing through normal probate hassles

Potential tax consequences are something most people don't think about. One solution to consider is a life insurance policy. Life insurance can be one of the most efficient means for your clients to leave a legacy.


It's estimated that over the next several decades, a wealth transfer of roughly
$30 trillion will pass from one generation to the next.*

Americo offers Lifetime Legacy, a specially-designed universal life product for the Senior Market to help address this need. By positioning assets that are not being used for income into a life insurance policy, a larger income tax-free benefit is available. Lifetime Legacy offers a way for your clients to leave their money on a tax-favored basis, plus potentially avoid the costs and delays of probate.

Lifetime Legacy may also provide your clients a way to effectively leverage the money they plan to pass on. By positioning even some assets into Lifetime Legacy, your clients can immediately increase their estate value.
